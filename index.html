Description of our products

A new form of gardening tool is self-refilling plant pots, a type of container with an automatic filling system that keeps your plants watered while requiring minimal time and attention. Self-filling plant pots can include moisture sensors, which water the plant when the moisture of the soil gets low.

They allow you to eliminate the stress of overwatering. When the moisture level begins to go down, the smart self-watering pot will draw from a storage tank. These pots are ideal for anyone whose life is very busy or someone with less experience with watering plants.

In addition, it cuts back the time needed to tend to the plants each day. These pots can also be more sustainable as they will utilize a controlled release system to more responsibly manage water. To sum up, a self-refilling plant pot simplifies gardening with smart technology to not only make your plants happy and healthy but also more sustainable on our planet.

How it works

The pot's internal sensor monitors soil moisture and automatically triggers water release from the built-in reservoir when levels drop below the ideal point. Excess water drains safely, preventing overhydration and ensuring your plants always get exactly what they need.
